About YassChat

YassChat is the result of a journey that began during my undergraduate studies. Originally conceived as a graduation project in Java, the application has now undergone a transformation, leveraging the skills and knowledge acquired through the ALX Software Engineering Program. This reimagination of YassChat utilizes Flask for the backend and JavaScript for the frontend, creating a real-time chat application that not only reflects the evolution of technology but also showcases my growth as a software developer. The goal remains the same: to provide users with a seamless and secure communication experience.
